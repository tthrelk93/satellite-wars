- Architecture C61 showed the stronger `26.25°` receiver containment is a real middle state: it improves the sign defect and broad quick-shape metrics, but it pushes burden poleward into the `33.75°` shoulder and NH ocean upper-cloud maintenance.
- This experiment holds the active C60 `18.75°` transition preserve and `26.25°` receiver containment fixed and only adds a local `33.75°` poleward-shoulder carryover containment carveout.
- The bounded question is whether a narrow shoulder carveout can recapture the NH ocean rebound while preserving the export-side relief from C60.
