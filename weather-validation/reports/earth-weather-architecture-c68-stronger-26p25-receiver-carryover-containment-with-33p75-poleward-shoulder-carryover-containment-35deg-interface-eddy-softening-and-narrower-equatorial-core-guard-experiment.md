## Interpretation

- Architecture C67 showed that C66 is beneficial but still paid for by the upper equatorial eddy branch and the outer transition / shoulder flux rows.
- This experiment keeps the C66 family intact and changes only the latitude reach of the equatorial guard, which makes it a clean geometry test instead of a new mechanism family.
- The bounded question is whether pulling the guard inward toward the equatorial core can keep the lower/mid relief while preventing the repayment from landing at `11.25°`, `18.75°`, `26.25°`, and `33.75°`.
